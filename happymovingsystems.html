Happy Moving Systems

Moving company

A Wordpress website.
The client asked for the site to be clean modern responsive and fast.
With a form and a map on the main page.

Implementing the project was pretty simple and straight forward. The main things I had to customize were the form and the accessibility widget.

The design:
The company name is very long and I would usually stack the words on top of each other for the logo However I wanted the site to have a thin header for a cleaner and modern look.
The arrow goes from a to in and is both a smile and a road.
The blue colors represent trust which was very important for the customer.
